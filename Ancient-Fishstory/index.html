Ancient Fishstory is a roguelike hybrid about exploration, evolution, and storytelling.

Game

Choose a sea creature and embark on a quest of epic proportions!

Strike out in search of treasure, explore the depths, and seek renown,
or stick around and build(or destroy!) an underwater ecosystem.

Adapt and evolve to your surroundings, creating a coherent story of a family tree of many generations over eons that you can return to as many times as you want and trace through the years.



Download Demo!

Features

Large Detailed Environments

Persistent, Meaningful AI and Events - Everything happens for a reason.

Detailed Player Customization
